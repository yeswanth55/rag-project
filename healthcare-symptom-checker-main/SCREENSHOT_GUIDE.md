## How to Take Screenshots

### Windows:
- **Snipping Tool**: Search "Snipping Tool" in Start Menu
- **Snip & Sketch**: Win + Shift + S
- **Full Screen**: Win + PrtScn

Create a folder: `d:\Unthinkable\screenshots\`
